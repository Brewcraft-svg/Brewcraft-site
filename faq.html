Delivery

First orders take 3–4 weeks from artwork approval to delivery. This includes production time at our UK supplier and shipping. Repeat orders using existing artwork are faster.

We deliver anywhere in the UK. Cups are shipped directly from our supplier to your café — no middleman confusion. We track the order and follow up after delivery.

Payment

Payment is required upfront before production begins. We send an invoice once you've approved your artwork proof. We accept bank transfer and most major payment methods.

All prices shown on this site are exclusive of VAT. VAT will be added to your invoice at the standard UK rate where applicable. Get in touch if you have any questions about this.
