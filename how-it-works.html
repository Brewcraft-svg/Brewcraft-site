From quote to delivered in five steps.

Every order follows the same clear process. No grey areas, no surprises — just your cups, done right.

STEP 01 Within 24 hours

Get a free quote

Fill in the contact form or drop us an email with your cup size, quantity, and a bit about your brand. We'll come back to you within 24 hours with a clear, no-surprises quote.

STEP 02 We handle the artwork

Share your branding

Send over your logo and any brand guidelines — colours, fonts, preferences. No print-ready files needed. We handle all the artwork prep and formatting ourselves.

STEP 03 Your sign-off required

Approve your proof

We'll send you a digital proof showing exactly how your cup will look — logo, colours, QR code and all. Nothing goes to production until you've signed it off.

STEP 04 3–4 weeks from approval

We handle production

Once approved, we place the order and manage production from start to finish. We'll keep you updated and confirm your expected delivery date.

STEP 05 Reorder reminders included

Delivered to your café

Your branded cups arrive directly to your door. We keep track of your stock and reach out before you run low — so you're never caught out mid-service.
