The process

Simple from first contact
to your door.

Five steps. No jargon. A real person handling your order at every stage.

01
Day 1

Get a quote

Tell us your cup size (8oz or 12oz), how many you need, and a rough idea of your design. We'll come back to you within 24 hours with a full quote — no commitment required at this stage.

02
Days 2–5

Artwork coordination

Once you're happy with the quote, we collect your artwork files — logo, colours, any design direction. We prepare everything for print and send you a proof to review. Nothing goes to production without your explicit sign-off.

03
On approval

Invoice & payment

Once artwork is approved we send your invoice. Payment is required upfront before production begins. We accept bank transfer and most major payment methods. The £35 design setup fee is included in your first order invoice.

04
Weeks 1–4

Production

Your order goes to our trusted UK supplier. We manage the relationship so you don't have to. First orders take 3–4 weeks. Repeat orders using the same artwork are faster. We'll keep you updated throughout.

05
Delivery

Delivered to your café

Cups are delivered directly to your café — no middleman confusion. We follow up after delivery to make sure everything arrived correctly and in perfect condition. Then we'll set a reminder for your next reorder.
